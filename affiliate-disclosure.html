The Short Version

BrickPick earns a small commission when you click our links and make a purchase — at absolutely no extra cost to you. This is how we keep the site free. Our recommendations are always based on what we genuinely think is the best choice, never on commission rates.

Last updated: January 1, 2025

What Is Affiliate Marketing?

When we link to a product on Amazon or another retailer, we use a special tracking link. If you click that link and buy something — even a completely different product — we receive a small percentage of the sale as a referral fee.

This is called affiliate marketing. It is a standard, legitimate way for websites to earn income without charging visitors or displaying intrusive ads. The price you pay is identical to what you'd pay by going to Amazon directly.

How It Affects Our Reviews

We want to be crystal clear: affiliate commissions do not influence our recommendations.

  • We recommend products we genuinely believe are the best for each category
  • We never recommend a product solely because it pays a higher commission
  • We include negative aspects and limitations in our assessments
  • Our editorial content is written independently of any commercial relationships

If we wouldn't recommend a product to a friend, we won't recommend it to you.

This disclosure is clearly linked from every page of BrickPick, including next to all affiliate links.
